# 🌍 Multi-City Travel & Expense Reimbursement Tracker

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Multi-City Travel & Expense Reimbursement Tracker** is an Excel-based finance automation solution designed to streamline employee travel claim verification and reimbursement processing. Organizations often struggle to validate travel expenses manually, especially when reimbursement policies vary by city and allowance category.

This project automates the verification process by comparing employee claims against predefined city-wise allowance limits, instantly identifying eligible reimbursements and flagging exceptions for review.

---

# 🚀 Business Problem

Managing travel reimbursements is a common challenge for finance and administration teams. Employees frequently submit travel claims for accommodation, meals, transportation, and other business expenses.

Manual verification creates several issues:

* Time-consuming claim reviews
* Inconsistent approval decisions
* Difficulty enforcing company travel policies
* Increased risk of overpayments
* Delays in reimbursement processing
* Limited visibility into policy violations

As employee travel volume grows, maintaining compliance becomes increasingly difficult.

---

# 💡 Solution

To solve this challenge, I developed a **Multi-City Travel & Expense Reimbursement Tracker** in Microsoft Excel.

The solution automatically:

✅ Validates expense claims against city-specific allowance limits

✅ Identifies approved reimbursement amounts

✅ Flags over-limit claims for review

✅ Applies business rules consistently

✅ Reduces manual verification effort

✅ Generates finance-ready reimbursement summaries

The result is a faster, more transparent, and policy-compliant reimbursement process.
